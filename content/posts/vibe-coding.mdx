# The Rise of Autonomous Sourcing

Capital formation is splitting into two high-friction jobs: investors must see every promising builder the moment they surface, and founders must map investor theses faster than generic outreach scales. Static spreadsheets and one-off alerts no longer cut it.

## From dashboards to live agents

Autonomous sourcing treats the web as a stream, not a snapshot. Agents watch GitHub velocity, launch traction, and public social graphs while memory layers deduplicate every touch. The goal is simple—surface conviction before the market catches up.

## Why local models matter

Running reasoning close to your workspace keeps drafts, scoring logic, and sensitive diligences out of opaque shared prompts. Paired with Z.ai or GLM-5.1 for deeper fit calls, you get fast iteration without surrendering governance.

## What changes for operators

Teams graduate from weekly CSV merges to continuous loops: ingest, score, draft, review, send. Operators stay in the loop for approvals, but the agent carries the mechanical weight—exactly where Shadow Partner and Founder Mode are aimed next.
